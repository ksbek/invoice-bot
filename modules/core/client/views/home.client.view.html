Use basic commands to craft beautifully smart invoices through Jimmy, our highly organized chat bot. Jimmy uses machine learning to automagically fetch client and sales data, chase overdue invoices and track payments on demand.

Automation

Utilize a streamlined approach to bot invoicing. Monitor and manage your whole business pipeline with intelligent insights. Proactively follow sales and accounting data to identify emerging revenue patterns.

Idiosyncratic

Our goal is to help you get paid faster. As such, all invoices are uniquely set to be now due from the day it is sent. This means the invoice due date status will appear as nowdue for a nominated period between 0 to 30+ days before becoming overdue.
